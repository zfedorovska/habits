Step 1. Make it invisible

Remove the cues of your bad habit from your environment

Step 2. Make it unattractive

Reframe your mind-set.

Highlight the benefits of avoiding your bad habits

Step 3. Make it difficult

Increase friction. Increase number of steps between you and your bad habits.

Use a commitment device. Restrict your future choices to the ones that benefit you.

Step 4. Make it unsatisfying

Get an accountability partner. Ask someone to watch your behavior.

Create a habit contract. Make the costs of your bad habit public and painful.
